FERRARI CECILIA
PhD Candidate in Astroparticle Physics

I am interested in detector R&D for Beyond Standard Model physics experiments. I have experience in neutrino mass physics, nuclear physics in ultra-low-background experiments, WIMP and light dark matter searches. I have characterized and refined pulse processing tools for several low-temperature devices, including multiplexed TESs, ABALONE prototype, and SiPMs. Additionally, I have conducted several data analysis and sensitivity studies.
